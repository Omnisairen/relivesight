Project Logs

Playlist central! Sometimes I get an idea for a playlist theme, throw some tracks together in Audacity, make the whole file sound like shit, and then upload it here.

Most of these are 'themed' in some way, but there will be semi-regular mixes where I'll just be putting out whatever good shit I'm listening to at the minute.
